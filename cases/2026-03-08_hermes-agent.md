## IC Thesis (≤100 words)

> Hermes Agent occupies a credible niche in structured output and function-calling model fine-tuning, with meaningful Hugging Face traction and integration citations across the open-source agent ecosystem. Nous Research's community standing is genuine. The risk is commoditisation: as frontier models improve native function-calling, the differentiation of fine-tuned open-weight alternatives narrows. Watch / Track — revisit if Nous Research announces enterprise tooling, a deployment platform, or a funding round that signals a transition from model publisher to infrastructure company.
