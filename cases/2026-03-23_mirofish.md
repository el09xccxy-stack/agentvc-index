## Dimension Scores

### A. Ecosystem Health (25%) — Score: 6.5

Stars growth explosive (+13K in 7 days) but classic "viral spike" pattern. Extremely new project. Contributor base narrow — essentially one developer plus Shanda backing. Community forming primarily in China. Forks healthy (~1,900) but sustained contribution unproven.

### B. Team & Governance (20%) — Score: 4.5

Solo student founder with no production software track record. Shanda investment thesis was "super-individual" narrative, not deep tech DD. No visible engineering leadership team. Governance undefined. High single-point-of-failure risk.

### C. Technical Moat (20%) — Score: 5.0

Core simulation engine is OASIS (CAMEL-AI), not proprietary — MiroFish adds product layer on top. Five-stage pipeline is well-designed but architecturally replicable. Key moat question: can simulations produce genuinely predictive results? Currently unvalidated.

### D. Product-Market Fit (20%) — Score: 5.5

Use cases compelling in theory: PR crisis simulation, policy forecasting, financial scenarios. Early demos impressive (Polymarket bot $4,266 profit / 338 trades). However, v0.1.2 is pre-PMF. No enterprise adoption, no pricing model. Viral attention is curiosity-driven.

### E. Exit Potential (15%) — Score: 6.0

Shanda backing provides runway. M&A interest plausible from consulting firms or prediction market companies. IPO path extremely distant.

---

## Composite Score: 5.54 → 🟠 Watch

**Watch Conditions:** Highest social media buzz in W13 but weakest investment fundamentals. Revisit in 60 days: (a) team expansion, (b) enterprise pilot customers, (c) prediction accuracy benchmark validation.
