## IC Thesis (≤100 words)

> DeerFlow is a technically credible multi-agent deep research framework backed by ByteDance engineering. Its modular pipeline architecture and strong initial traction position it as a meaningful player in the agentic workflow space. The primary risk is corporate dependency: without an independent commercial entity or licensing model, the exit path and PMF signals are structurally constrained. Recommend with Conditions — watch for spin-out signals, independent deployment cases, or enterprise licensing announcements that would de-risk the corporate-OSS overhang.
