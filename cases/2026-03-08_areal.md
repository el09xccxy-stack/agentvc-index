## IC Thesis (≤100 words)

> Areal is positioned at the intersection of two high-momentum trends: open-source LLM training and reinforcement learning for reasoning improvement. The RLVR methodology is technically credible and the timing is strong — post-DeepSeek-R1, the market understands the value of verifiable reward training. The gap is commercialisation: InclusionAI has not yet demonstrated a path from research framework to enterprise product. Recommend with Conditions — the technical thesis is sound and the exit path to model labs or enterprise AI platforms is plausible; watch for a product launch, enterprise pilot, or funding announcement.
