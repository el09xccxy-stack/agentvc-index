## IC Thesis (≤100 words)

> Agent Vault addresses a critical bottleneck in the AI Agent stack: secure credential management in non-deterministic environments. By utilizing a proxy-based brokered access model, it mitigates prompt injection risks without exposing secrets to the agent. Backed by Infisical’s proven Series A team and $1.7M ARR commercial engine, the project has a high GTM floor. While current community health metrics are weak (low external contribution), its strategic positioning as a governance layer makes it a prime acquisition target for cloud giants. Recommend with conditions: monitor for organic developer adoption and production-grade forks over the next 6 months.
