## IC Thesis (≤100 words)

> AgentScope offers a technically sophisticated multi-agent framework with a defensible actor-based runtime architecture and strong Alibaba ecosystem backing. The distributed agent execution model and fault-tolerant message passing represent genuine technical differentiation beyond prompt orchestration. Commercial risk mirrors DeerFlow: corporate OSS origin limits independent PMF evidence. Recommend with Conditions — the framework is production-ready and the technical moat is real, but investability requires evidence of adoption outside the Alibaba cloud stack.
