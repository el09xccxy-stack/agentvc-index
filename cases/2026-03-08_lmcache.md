## IC Thesis (≤100 words)

> LMCache addresses a high-value infrastructure problem: reducing LLM inference cost and latency through intelligent KV-cache disaggregation and cross-instance reuse. The technical approach is non-trivial, measurable, and directly integrated with the vLLM ecosystem — the dominant open-source LLM serving stack. Academic origin provides credibility; the gap is commercial scaffolding. This is a strong Recommend with Conditions: the infrastructure thesis is sound and the exit path to cloud providers or LLM serving platforms is credible, pending evidence of enterprise deployment or a disclosed funding round.
